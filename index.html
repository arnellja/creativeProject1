Our Founder

Rodrigo moved to New York from Pisa, Spain back in 1880. He was so confused by this food that so many ate, with the same name as his hometown. After trying pizza, he fell in love with it and began his very own shop. It has been an upstate New York staple ever since.

Our Team Today

Today, we employ all locals in our great work of bringing quality pizza to the community. We are proud to announce we are an equal-opportunity employer, and work to improve every community that has welcomed us in!
